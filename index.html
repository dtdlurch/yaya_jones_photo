About Me

Hi, I'm Yvonne, but my friends call me Yaya.


A born and raised Georgia girl with a passion for capturing life's sweetest moments! I specialize in maternity, newborn, and family photography, creating timeless images that you and your loved ones can cherish forever. My goal is to provide a fun and relaxed experience, ensuring every session feels natural, fun, and filled with genuine connection. I can't wait to help you tell your story through beautiful, heartfelt photography!

Services

Simple, warm sessions designed to capture real moments — in-home or on-location.


Newborn

Capture the beauty of your newborn’s earliest days with a cozy in-home photography session. These sessions are designed to celebrate this special time in your life while allowing you to remain in the comfort of your own home. Expect the session to last 1–2 hours, giving plenty of time to work around your baby’s needs for feedings, cuddles, and soothing moments.

I’ll bring a variety of photography props to enhance the experience, including soft blankets, baskets, wraps, and accessories, so we can create timeless images that reflect your family’s unique style. Together, we’ll document the love, joy, and tiny details of your newest addition in a way that feels effortless and natural…all from the comfort of your home.

Maternity

Celebrate the beauty of motherhood and the miracle of new life with a maternity session designed to honor your unique journey. Whether you’re glowing with your first baby or adding to your growing family, these sessions are all about capturing your connection, strength, and the anticipation of what’s to come.

We’ll choose a location that fits your vision—outdoors in golden light or indoors with cozy, intimate vibes—and take a mix of classic portraits and authentic moments. Partners, children, and pets are always welcome to join! Let’s create something timeless to remember this chapter forever.

Family

Capture the essence of your family's unique bond with a relaxed and fun photography session. Join me in creating timeless memories filled with laughter and genuine moments. From playful interactions to candid embraces, let's tell your family's story through images that reflect your love and connection. Whether it's a stroll in the park, a cozy afternoon at home, or a scenic outdoor adventure, I'll ensure your session is enjoyable and stress-free, resulting in beautiful photographs you'll cherish for years to come.

Creative Projects

Have an idea that doesn’t fit the usual boxes? Let’s make something special. Creative Projects are for styled shoots, themed sessions, collaborations, or anything imaginative—spooky graveyard portraits, bold editorial vibes, best‑friends sessions, brand concepts, and more.

We’ll brainstorm together, pick a location and look, and create images that feel unique, expressive, and fun.
