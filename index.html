About Me

Hi, I'm Yvonne, but my friends call me Yaya.


A born and raised Georgia girl with a passion for capturing life's sweetest moments! I specialize in maternity, newborn, and family photography, creating timeless images that you and your loved ones can cherish forever. My goal is to provide a fun and relaxed experience, ensuring every session feels natural, fun, and filled with genuine connection. I can't wait to help you tell your story through beautiful, heartfelt photography!
